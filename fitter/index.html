Privacy Policy for Fiziora

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Fiziora ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile fitness application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile photo (when you create an account or sign in with Google/Apple)
  • Authentication Data: User ID, authentication tokens, and login credentials
  • Profile Information: Name, fitness goals, and preferences you choose to share
  • Subscription Information: Payment details and subscription status (processed through third-party payment providers)

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:

  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and app performance metrics
  • Preferences: Dark mode settings, language preferences, and onboarding completion status

Health and Fitness Data

The App may collect health and fitness-related information:

  • Exercise Data: Workout history, exercise categories selected, and training plans
  • Fitness Goals: Goals you set and progress tracking information
  • Activity Data: Exercise preferences and fitness level information

Service Provision

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Deliver personalized workout plans and exercise recommendations
  • Process subscription payments and manage premium features

Communication

  • Send you important updates about the App
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Provide customer service and technical support

App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Conduct research and analytics to better understand user needs

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraud
